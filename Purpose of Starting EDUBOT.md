Purpose of Starting EDUBOT
Core Problem

Education systems—especially in India—face structural challenges:

Teacher shortage (especially in rural/semi-urban areas)
Inconsistent teaching quality
Limited individual attention
Low spoken English exposure
Manual, non-data-driven teaching methods
No real-time feedback loop for student understanding

Even with ERP systems and digital content:

Schools manage administration well, but learning outcomes remain inconsistent.

Purpose Statement

EDUBOT was created to:

Ensure consistent, high-quality, AI-assisted academic delivery for every student, regardless of location or teacher availability.

It is not replacing teachers.
It is augmenting, stabilizing, and scaling education quality.

2. What Problem EDUBOT Solves
Problem Breakdown
1. Teaching Quality Variability

Different teachers → different outcomes.

2. Lack of Individual Attention

One teacher → 30–50 students → no personalization.

3. Weak Concept Reinforcement

Students fall behind → no structured recovery.

4. No Continuous Assessment

Testing is periodic, not continuous.

5. Spoken English Gap

Students understand theory but cannot communicate.

EDUBOT Solution

EDUBOT introduces:

Standardized AI teaching
24/7 doubt solving
Continuous assessment
Automatic remediation
Daily spoken English practice
